Lane Detection Module

Camera-based lane detection module. The module will take a video stream in a format that is used in autonomous systems as input, and it will produce annotated video with lane area. We will split the video into frames, and process it as an image in ROS(Robot Operating System)

The image frame goes through the following processing procedure:
  1. Gradient threshold processing will detect interesting features (e.g: lanes) in the frame and output a binary image (white & black)
  2. Color threshold processing will detect lanes in the frame by extracting white and yellow pixels (common lane marking colors in the US)
  3. Perspective transformation to brid's eye view, since the distance of pixels on the image will be proportional to the actual distance thus more useful for controlling the vehicle
  4. Lane fitting by calculating where pixels are most dense after seperating the lane pixels from the background
