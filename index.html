About Me

My name is Sidiq Kaddo and I chose to be an engineer because I’ve always loved to build things and aspired to make an impact in the world. Attending one of the top computer engineering schools nationwide put me at an advantage as I’m confident that I’m getting the best education in my field. I’ve also had opportunities to develop and deploy software for my university research and at my internship at Dish Network. My work experience gave me an insight into the technology industry and the various software development methodologies used in building applications.

Being an engineer means being able to come up with solutions to real life problems and having worked on many projects gave me a chance to apply the knowledge I learned in school. I'm seeking a platform where I can create a solution to a problem that many thought is impossible to solve. I believe that the steps that i've taken will lead me to opportunities that will push me to my full potential.
